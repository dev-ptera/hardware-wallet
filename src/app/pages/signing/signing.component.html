About Message Signing
A private key asocciated with an account can be used to sign a message and generate a digital signature. The generated digital signature can be used by anyone to verify the authenticity and integrity of the message. Signing messages is a very useful method for verifying your identity without compromising your private key and putting your funds at risk.
Message Signing
For security reasons, a message to be signed must start with "message-", and must not be exactly 32 characters long.

Block Signing
WARNING: Signing blocks is DANGEROUS. If you sign a block whose purpose and actions are unknown to you, it may result in a LOSS OF FUNDS. Only sign blocks when you know what they are doing and you trust its source. Moreover, be cautious not to share the generated block signature, as it will allow anyone who has access to it, to broadcast the signed block and thus PERFORMING A TRANSACTION ON YOUR BEHALF.
A transaction block can be signed and manually submitted (broadcasted) to the network to be proccessed, propagated and confirmed.
